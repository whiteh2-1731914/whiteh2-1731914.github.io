Cat's and their Kittens

By Hailey White

If you're like me and you just absolutely love cats, you've come to the right place. Here, you will be able to find all kinds of information on different cat breads, while enjoy cute pictures and videos of others' cats as well as my very own. I hope you enjoy your journey!

Personal Expertise

From the twenty-one year's I have lived on this Earth, I have always had a pet cat. It started out with an all black-colored fur cat that my parents had named Midnight. Then came Tommy, who was bought for my older sister when my younger brother was born. Boots was the neect cat my family adopted, followed by Panther. In more recent years, we adopted a kitten named Olive, Coti, Nova, and the newest kitten's, Frankie and Lester in which are still with us today.

Personal References

I would like to introduce to you the beginning of a fun journey to meet the cats the my family has adopted in the recent years! We have Olive, who my brother decided to surprise my family with after he had surgery to fix his torn ACL and LCL. She is the black and white adult cat, and has what looks like white boots on. She is a sassy girl who loves attention, but only for a certain amount of time, when SHE wants to, and she can be a bit skiddish. Next, we have Coti. She is the dark orange adult cat with the corkscrew tail. She was adopted into our family by my older sister as well as her husband. She is a bit timid, but loves her attention from her mom and dad. Then we have Nova, the white-pawed, light and dark-brown mix of colored fur male adolescent cat. He was brought home as a surprise by my younger brother after his friend's adult cat had a litter. He is energetic, loves the outdoors, and is the most relaxed cat. The newest addition to our cat family is Frankie and Lester, two kittens from the same litter. Frankie closley resembles the look of Nova, while Lester is all black. Frankie is a bit younger than Lester, and so far seems way more outgoing. Lester seems to show a bit of anxiety when it comes to meeting newes people.
